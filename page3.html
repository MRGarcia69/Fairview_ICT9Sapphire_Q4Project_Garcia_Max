BGC

Bonifacio Global City, or BGC for short, is a bustling neighborhood in Metro Manila, Philippines. Recognized for its contemporary architecture, vibrant atmosphere, and busy business districts, BGC is a center of activity that mixes residential buildings, retail centers, restaurants, and green areas with corporate headquarters.

BORACAY

Nestled in the Philippines, Boracay is a well-known tropical paradise known for its immaculate white sand beaches, glistening waters, and abundant marine life. Travelers from all over the world looking for adventure and relaxation come to this lovely hideaway island getaway in the Western Visayas region.

BANAUE RICE TERRACES

These terraces demonstrate a complex and sustainable farming system that is still in use today. They were constructed by Ifugao ancestors using simple tools. The tiered environment, which is fed by mountain springs and streams and features an intricate irrigation system, is not only aesthetically pleasing but also a useful method of cultivating rice on steep mountain slopes.
